How Points Work
IPL 2026 · Kicket scoring
🎯
Slots
You have 11 slots to distribute across the 10 IPL teams. You choose how many slots each team gets.
🏆
Points per Win
Each match a team wins, you earn 10 points for every slot you have on that team.
🔥
Streak Bonus
Same team wins back to back? Bonus kicks in!
Win 1 → 10 pts · Win 2 → 15 pts · Win 3 → 20 pts · Win 4 → 25 pts (keeps going +5 per win)
💔
Streak Breaks
Team loses → streak resets. Back to 10 pts per slot next win.
🔄
Daily Swap
Once per matchday, move exactly 1 slot from one team to another. Swap window: midnight to noon IST on match days.
✅
No Negative Points
Team loses = 0 points. Never penalised.

How to Play
It’s not about prediction. It’s about decision.
1. Create or join a Clubhouse
Start a private space for your people or join one with a code.
2. Assign your picks
You have 11 picks. Place them across teams wisely.
3. Manage your swaps
You get one swap every matchday.
4. Play the season
Earn points as your teams win. Bonus points for winning streaks.
5. Rule your WhatsApp circle
Climb the Leaderboard for ultimate bragging rights.
6. Join before 28.03 | 12:00 IST
